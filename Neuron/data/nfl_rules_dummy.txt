NFL RULES DUMMY DATA
====================

SCORING
-------
- Touchdown: 6 points
- Extra Point (Kick): 1 point
- Two-Point Conversion: 2 points
- Field Goal: 3 points
- Safety: 2 points

GAMEPLAY
--------
- 4 downs to advance 10 yards
- 60 minutes of play (4 quarters of 15 minutes)
- Teams switch sides after each quarter
- Overtime: 10 minutes in regular season

POSITIONS
---------
- Quarterback (QB): Throws the ball
- Running Back (RB): Rushes the ball
- Wide Receiver (WR): Catches passes
- Tight End (TE): Blocks and catches
- Offensive Line: Protects QB

PENALTIES
---------
- Holding: 10 yards
- Pass Interference: Spot of foul
- False Start: 5 yards
- Offsides: 5 yards
- Roughing the Passer: 15 yards

FANTASY RELEVANCE
-----------------
- Standard scoring: 1 point per 10 rushing yards
- PPR: 1 point per reception
- Passing TD: 4 points (typically)
- Rushing/Receiving TD: 6 points
